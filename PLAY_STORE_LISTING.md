## Full Description (4000 characters max)

RV Nomad is the ultimate companion for RV travelers, campers, and road trippers. Whether you're a full-time nomad or a weekend warrior, RV Nomad helps you find the perfect campground, plan your route, and save money on every trip.

**FIND YOUR PERFECT CAMPSITE**
Browse over 3,300 campgrounds across all 50 states and Canada. Filter by state, national, and provincial parks, RV parks, military campgrounds, and free boondocking spots. Search by amenities, hookup type, amp service, RV size compatibility, pet-friendliness, and more. Every campground includes photos, detailed amenities, user reviews, and estimated nightly rates.

**AI-POWERED TRIP PLANNING**
Tell our AI trip planner where you're going, how long you want to travel, your budget, and your RV size — and it builds a complete day-by-day itinerary with campground recommendations, driving distances, and things to do at each stop. Don't like certain campground brands? Exclude KOA, Jellystone, or any chain with one tap.

**SAVE MONEY WITH DISCOUNT STACKER**
Stack multiple camping memberships and discounts to find the cheapest rate at every campground. Supports Good Sam, Passport America, Harvest Hosts, Thousand Trails, KOA Value Kard, military discounts, senior discounts, and more. See exactly how much you save.

**ADA ACCESSIBLE CAMPGROUND FINDER**
Find campgrounds with ADA accessible sites, facilities, and equipment. Locate nearby track chair programs at national and state parks within 75 miles of any campsite, with directions and availability info.

**NEARBY SERVICES**
Tap any campsite to see the nearest fuel stations with diesel and gas prices, camping supply stores (Walmart, Camping World, Bass Pro, Tractor Supply), and RV repair shops with phone numbers and emergency availability. Get directions from the campsite to any service.

**COMPARE CAMPGROUNDS SIDE-BY-SIDE**
Can't decide between two campgrounds? Compare them side by side — amenities, prices, reviews, hookups, cell signal, noise level, and more.

**RV WEIGHT & SCALE FINDER**
Track your RV's weight with the built-in calculator and find the nearest CAT scales and weigh stations along your route.

**CANCELLATION SCANNER**
Monitor campgrounds for cancellation openings so you can grab a spot at popular parks that are usually booked solid.

**RV PROBLEMS & RECALLS**
Search NHTSA recall data by manufacturer, look up your VIN, and browse common RV problems with prevention tips and repair cost estimates.

**CAMPGROUND REVIEWS**
Read honest reviews from real RV travelers. See ratings, rig types, and helpful tips from fellow campers.

**STATE-BY-STATE RV LAWS**
Know the rules before you go. Every state includes RV-specific laws, boondocking regulations, and overnight parking rules.

**ADDITIONAL FEATURES**
• Hiking trail finder near campgrounds
• Fuel price tracker with RV-friendly stations
• Offline access to saved campgrounds
• Dark mode support
• Works on phones and tablets

Download RV Nomad today and start exploring!
